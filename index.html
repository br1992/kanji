Welcome to Kanji no Renshuu!

This web app will allow you to expand and test your knowledge of kanji readings. To begin, create a new text file in a simple text editor such as TextPad (Windows) or TextEdit (Mac). Next, type sets of kanji and its readings in hiragana and romaji. Here's an example:

面接　めんせつ　mensetsu
成績　せいせき　seiseki
性格　せいかく　seikaku

Because there the reading is provided in both hiragana and romaji, you may aswer the quiz with either, Now, save the file in UTF-8 .txt format. That's it. Go to the next page to upload your file. Once you've done that, you can pratice flashcards and take quizes to your hearts content!
